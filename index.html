Platform Glimpse

AI Training

AI Training System is our method for evaluating participants on an individual level. It gives us the ability to provide customized solutions based on specific needs. The result is much more than an exercise program; it is a purpose-driven system. In this system a participant's all the activities are tracked down by the AI including the diet. Than the AI system generates an customisable diet and workout plan for the individual which is suitable for them. The AI keeps a watch on the participant and help them reach their desired goals

AI Diet

The AI diet is a special feature of the platform which takes height, weight, body type and desired health goal as input from the user and designs a calory efficient or calory deficient for one's desired goal. It is customisable for the user accordingly.
